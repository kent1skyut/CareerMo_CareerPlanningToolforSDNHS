ABOUT

Choosing the right career is a decision that goes far beyond simply selecting a job; it's about finding a path that aligns with one's passions, interests, strengths, and long-term goals. It's about discovering the path that will not only lead to professional success but also personal fulfillment and happiness. CareerMo was develop to empower high school students to make informed and confident decisions about their futures.

"Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life." - Confucius

How this system Works?

Our decision support system (DSS) for career planning works by first assessing high school students' skills and interests through a questionnaire. This data is then processed using a decision tree algorithm, which analyzes their responses and identifies potential career paths that align with their abilities and preferences. Based on this analysis, our system generates personalized career recommendations for each student, helping them make informed decisions about their future.
